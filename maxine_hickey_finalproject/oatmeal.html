Oatmeal

Ingredients:
- 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats, or oats of choice
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup water
- 1/8 tsp kosher salt
- 1.2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 tsp honey
- Add whatever topping(s) you want

Recipe:
1. Combine oats, milk, water, salt, and cinnamon in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat
2. Simmer uncovered for 3-5 minutes until thicken, tiring occasionally. Remove from heat and let cool slightly
3. Pour into a bowl, add desired toppings, and serve
