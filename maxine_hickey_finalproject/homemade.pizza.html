Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:
Homemade dough if you don’t purchase it premade:
- 1 1/4 cups lukewarm water
- 1 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 packet active dry yeast
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 c extra virgin olive oil

Toppings:
- Extra-virgin olive oil as needed
- 1/4 cup cornmeal, divided
- Add whatever sauce(s) and topping(s) you want

Recipe:
1. Grease large bowl with cooking spray
2. In small bowl ass water and sugar. Stir to dissolve
3. Add yeast and wait 8 minutes
4. In another large bowl, add flour, salt, and oil
5. Pour in yeast mixture, then stir with wooden spoon until dough starts to form
6. Knead dough into bowl, adding flour as needed
7. Place dough ball into greased bowl, cover it, put it in a warm spot and wait until it is doubled in size for 90 minutes
8. Roll out dough, transfer to baking sheet, add toppings
9. Bake until crust is golden and cheese is melt, about 15 minutes
