Eggs Benedict

Ingredients:
Hollandaise Sauce (if you want homemade):
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 cup butter
Eggs Benedict:
- 3 English muffins
- 3 tbsp butter, softened
- 1 tsp butter
- 6 thin slices Canadian Bacon
- 6 eggs
- 4 tsp distilled white vinegar

Recipe:
1. Fry Canadian bacon in skillet over medium-high heat
2. In large saucepan, add 3 inches of water and bring to a simmer
3. Pour in vinegar
4. Break eggs into water and cook for 2-3 minutes. Remove eggs with a slotted spoon
5. Melt butter
6. Blend Hollandaise sauce ingredients together in a blender. Add half of the hot butter, then blend in the lemon juice, then blend in the remaining butter
7. Serve eggs on English muffins. Top with Canadian Bacon and drizzle Hollandaise sauce
